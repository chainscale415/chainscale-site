Services

Every engagement is
built to outlast us.

We build the GTM infrastructure, playbooks, and commercial frameworks that run without us in the room. No dependency. Just capability.

01
GTM Strategy & Foundation
Core Advisory

The strategic foundation every other GTM motion depends on. We define who you sell to, why they buy, and how you beat the alternatives — with specificity that holds up in a live sales call.

  • Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) definition by product tier
  • Current state assessment and gap analysis
  • Competitive landscape and battle card development
  • Enterprise narrative and positioning framework
  • Metrics framework and KPI dashboard specification
02
White-Label GTM
Specialty

White-label sales motions are a distinct discipline. Partner pricing, liquidity structures, compliance handoffs, and captive customer economics require frameworks built specifically for platform businesses.

  • White-label ICP with captive customer base filter
  • Integration tier pricing strategy and walk-away framework
  • Partner sales deck, one-pager, and comparison sheet
  • Objection handling for 'build vs. buy' and pricing pressure
  • Geographic sequencing and pipeline prioritization
03
Sales Enablement & Pipeline
Execution

The tools, templates, and playbooks your sales team needs to move fast without breaking process. Built for enterprise close rates, not startup hustle.

  • Sales process map from qualification to close
  • Outreach sequences and email template libraries
  • Discovery call guides and qualification scorecards
  • Prospect list development with geographic sequencing
  • Live deal coaching and pipeline dashboard reporting
04
Commercial Model Design
Financial

Revenue modeling that connects deal economics to hiring decisions. We build the financial frameworks that let founders make confident bets rather than gut-feel calls.

  • Revenue per deal model by integration or product tier
  • Conservative, base, and optimistic scenario analysis
  • Hire trigger threshold tied to signed ARR
  • Pipeline math: deals needed to reach scale milestones
  • Pricing floor and walk-away analysis by deal type
05
Marketing Infrastructure
Demand Gen

Most early-stage B2B companies have a marketing gap — founder-led outbound with no inbound engine. We assess what exists, audit what's not working, and design the demand infrastructure that scales.

  • Marketing and agency audit with gap analysis
  • Channel prioritization and content strategy
  • CRM evaluation and implementation roadmap
  • Website positioning and conversion brief
  • Build vs. hire vs. agency framework
06
Crypto & Web3 GTM
Domain Expertise

Selling into crypto-native buyers requires a different vocabulary, a different trust framework, and a different understanding of where decisions actually get made. We bring that fluency natively.

  • Crypto exchange and custodian partnership strategy
  • Crypto-collateralized lending market positioning
  • Blockchain infrastructure and SaaS GTM frameworks
  • Regulatory and compliance narrative development
  • Community and ecosystem-led growth strategy

About

Operator-built.
Advisor-delivered.

ChainScale is the advisory practice of Scott Bambacigno — a GTM operator with deep roots in enterprise SaaS and the Bitcoin and blockchain ecosystem.

Scott
Bambacigno
Founder & Principal Advisor

Scott brings over a decade of enterprise go-to-market experience to companies building at the intersection of B2B SaaS and crypto infrastructure. Based in San Francisco since 2004, he has advised and operated across the full GTM stack — from ICP definition to sales playbook execution to commercial model design.

His advisory practice, ChainScale, focuses exclusively on companies where GTM complexity is high: white-label platforms, multi-tier pricing structures, crypto-native buyer audiences, and founder-led sales motions that need to be codified and scaled.

Scott is a long-term participant in the Bitcoin and blockchain community and brings genuine domain fluency — not surface-level familiarity — to engagements in the crypto infrastructure space.

Strategy without execution infrastructure is just a presentation. Every engagement ends with something that runs without me in the room.
